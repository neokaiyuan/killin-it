Welcome to Team Killin' It's Project Ideas

1. Support groups need better reach to individuals

Support groups need a way to communicate their difficulties and crises and to be supportive of each other when not meeting. Sharing snippets of their stories with each other can help the support process and mitigate feelings of being alone. The use-context would be when at home, away from therapy.

2. Remote family dinners

Families are increasingly strewn across the world, lacking the most common shared experience that families enjoy: dinner. Families should be able to have dinner together remotely without any feelings of being apart.

3. Remote drinking games

Sometimes your drinking buddies move away to far away places. This app helps you play drinking games with them remotely and prevents distance from getting in the way of good times.
